Festivals

Festivals we celebrate and ones we'd like to if we could ever get organized.

Winter (December, January, February)

  • Winter Solstice / Midwinter / Yule (about December 21st)
  • Christmas (December 25)
  • New Years Eve (December 31)
  • New Years Day (January 1)
  • Imbolc (February 1st)
  • Valentine's Day (February 14)
  • Sheila Day (February 22nd)

Spring (March, April, May)

  • Vernal Equinox / Ostara (usually March 20)
  • Easter (about April 20)
  • Darnold Day (April 28)
  • Beltane / May Day (May 1)

Summer (June, July, August)

  • Summer Solstice (Midsummer / Litha) (about June 21)
  • Independence Day (American)
  • Gortimer Day (July 26)
  • Lughnasadh (usually August 1)

Fall (September, October, November)

  • Autumnal Equinox / Mabon (September 22 or 23)
  • Halloween (October 31)
  • Mortimer Day (October 31)
  • Samhain (October 31-November 1)
  • Thanksgiving (American) (Fourth Thursday in November)
