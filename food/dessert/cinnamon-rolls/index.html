Overnight Holiday Cinnamon Rolls

At my parents bakery, they sold cinnamon rolls. We had both a commercially prepared one and we made them in house, usually in the off-season, when we had time to experiment. These aren't those, but they are pretty good. The icing comes from Leif Enger's Peace Like A River.
