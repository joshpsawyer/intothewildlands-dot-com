1-2-8 Honey Lemon Tea

Whenever somebody's sick, they get this tea. You can make it to taste, but I always use this ratio because there's a song called 1-2-8. This stuff tastes... great!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp (1 part) freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp (2 parts) honey (raw clover is particularly nice)
  • 1/2 cup (8 parts) water

Steps

  1. Get the water hot enough to dissolve the honey. PDT is about 140°F and you can drink it right away.
  2. Combine all ingredients and stir until the honey is dissolved.
  3. Serve immediately to your sick family member, maybe with some of Gortimer's Graham Cracker Sandwiches?
